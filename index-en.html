Seçkin BAŞKÖY

Hello, I opened my eyes to the world in 1976 in the beautiful district of Ordu, Korgan. Since my childhood, the purpose of my life has been Computer Programming and I have progressed in this field with great passion. In the 1990s I spent long hours working on desktop applications and programs, first with QBasic (DOS) and later with Visual Basic 6.

I developed my knowledge and skills further by successfully completing the Department of Accounting and Finance (undergraduate) at Anadolu University, Faculty of Business Administration, and then the Computer Programming Department of the Open Education Faculty of the same university. My web programming adventure, which started with ASP in 2001, continued by developing multi-user websites and applications with php and mysql. During this time, I worked as a web designer in the IT department of different units of Akdeniz University and undersigned important projects.

Over the years, I have worked in various companies in positions such as IT Manager, IT Manager, IT Manager, Web Design Team Leader. Most recently, in 2009, I worked as a Web Designer at Akdeniz University as a worker for years.

In May 2023, after a long and successful career, I retired. Now I work freely in my home office and share my experience and knowledge as a freelancer. I try to help those who have questions and I continue to do exciting work by taking part in new projects. In the field of Computer Programming, which I am passionate about all my life, I strive to produce eye-catching projects and to benefit people. I love what I do and I am excited to face a new challenge every day.

If you need a project too, don't hesitate to contact me! Together we can work to make your dreams come true.
